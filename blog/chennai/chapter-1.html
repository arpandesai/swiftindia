Alvin Varghese

November 26, 2016

Nayan Mujiadiya gave the introductory talk, he explained about the importance of organising meetups and building a Swift community in Chennai.

Later, Alvin Varghese took session regarding the importance of using Structs in Swift. He explained about Value Types, Immutable Semtantics, Using Generics with Structs, Subscripts in Structs, get and set inside Struct properties, Confirming Structs with Protocols and over all explanation by giving a practical example of "Creating a Matrix in Struct".

Thanks a lot to Paypal for giving out the space for organising the meetup and also for the refreshments.
